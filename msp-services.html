Managed Service Provider (MSP) Solutions

AWSCR8 offers comprehensive MSP services to keep your cloud and application environments secure, optimized, and running smoothly — 24/7. Our team blends global expertise with proven tools and processes to deliver tailored managed services aligned with your business goals and compliance needs.

🔹 24/7 Monitoring & Incident Response

Proactive monitoring via CrowdStrike, Microsoft Defender, and full VNOC services ensures rapid incident detection and response, minimizing downtime and protecting your assets.

🔹 Patch Management & Compliance

We manage patching and remediation for on-prem, cloud, and endpoint environments, helping you meet governance and compliance requirements efficiently.

🔹 Application-Specific Support

Dedicated support for applications we've developed, migrated, or specifically maintain — ensuring expert assistance when you need it most.

🔹 Identity & Access Management (IAM)

Robust SSO with Okta and Microsoft, MFA enforcement, CAC card integration, and Azure AD management across all cloud platforms to secure your user access.

🔹 Disaster Recovery Planning

Expert cloud-based disaster recovery planning ensures your critical systems can quickly recover and stay resilient against outages.

🔹 Security Services

Comprehensive security offerings including penetration testing, phishing simulations, vulnerability assessments, and compliance audits to protect your business.

🔹 Network Management

Management of VPNs and dedicated cloud connections to ensure secure, reliable network access for your operations.

Rightsizing, reserved instance planning, and on-demand VM selection—especially tailored for development environments—to reduce your cloud expenses.

🔹 Compliance Management

Guidance and support for HIPAA, SOC2, SSAE16, and GDPR compliance, including audits, policy development, and ongoing monitoring.

End-to-end DevOps pipeline management with Jenkins, GitHub Actions, and more — ensuring continuous delivery and operational efficiency.
